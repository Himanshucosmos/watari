# WATARI (渡り)

WATARI is a meditative web simulation where you guide a flock of birds through an endless ocean landscape. It was created out of a desire to build something quiet, reflective, and peaceful.

The experience is heavily inspired by the enduring legacy and music of Nujabes and the atmosphere of Samurai Champloo. As you fly, the simulation cycles through different weather and times of day—from bright mornings to quiet aurora nights—set entirely to an automated stream of his music.

### How to Experience

The simulation requires no installations. It relies on standard WebGL to render the infinite environment.

1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser.
2. Click "Take Flight" to begin.
3. Use the arrow keys to gently steer the lead bird.
4. Collect the glowing orbs scattered along the horizon. Collecting them will organically expand your flock over time.
5. You can control the music using the player in the bottom corner. Headphones are highly recommended.

There is no end goal, no high scores, and no way to fail. You can activate "Zen" mode using the on-screen buttons to let the flock navigate itself, allowing you to simply listen to the music and watch the world pass by. 

Take a breath, trust the wind, and enjoy the flight.
